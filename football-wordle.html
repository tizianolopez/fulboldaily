How to Play Football Wordle

Welcome to Fulbol Wordle! This football-themed wordle challenges your knowledge of football players.

Here are the basic rules:

  • Guess the hidden football player within six attempts.
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
  • Green indicates the letter is correct and in the right position.
  • Yellow indicates the letter is correct but in the wrong position.
  • Gray indicates the letter is not in the word.

Tips:

  • Think of common player names.
  • Use vowels and common consonants in your initial guesses to get more hints.

Enjoy the game and test your footy knowledge!

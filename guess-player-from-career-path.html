How to Play Guess the Player

Welcome to the Guess the Player from career game! Test your knowledge of football players' careers by identifying players based on their transfer history.

Here are the basic rules:

  • You will see a table with columns for "Season", "Team From", and "Team To".
  • Initially, the details in the table will be hidden.
  • Start by guessing the player based on the unknown career path.
  • If your guess is incorrect, or if you click "Skip", a new row (transfer) will be revealed
  • Your goal is to identify the player before all rows are revealed.

Tips:

  • Think of the well-known clubs to narrow down your guesses.
  • Pay close attention to the year the transfer was made, it's very useful!
  • A players first club might help you deduce his nationality
  • If a player has many transfers on the same year he might be Loaned
  • We show if a player is retired or not, use that to see if the player is still active

Enjoy the challenge and showcase your football trivia knowledge!

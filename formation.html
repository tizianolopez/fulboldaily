How to Play Starting 11 Formation

Welcome to the Starting 11 Formation game! This game challenges you to create a starting lineup with players from the given country.

Here are the basic rules:

  • You begin with an empty football formation.
  • A country flag will be displayed at the top of the screen.
  • Select a player from the given country to add to your formation.
  • After selecting a player, they will be placed into the formation, and a new flag will appear for the next player selection.
  • Repeat the process until you have filled all 11 positions in your lineup to win.
  • If you want to give up, click on the surrender icon. .

Tips:

  • Consider your overall formation (e.g., 4-4-2, 4-3-3) when selecting players.
  • Think about completing the hardest positions first to have an easier path later.
  • Familiarize yourself with players from each country to make informed selections.

Enjoy creating your ultimate starting 11 lineup!

How to Play the Crossword

Welcome to Cross-it! This football-themed crossword game challenges your knowledge of football-related terms and players.

Here are the basic rules:

  • Read the clues provided to identify football-related words.
  • Click on the boxes to fill in the crossword grid with the correct words.
  • Each word must fit both across and down based on the provided clues.
  • Use the keyboard or mouse to navigate between boxes.
  • If a word is correct it will automatically turn green (try as much as you want untill you get it right).

Tips:

  • Start with the clues you find the easiest, they can help you fill the other words.
  • Think of common football terms like players, team names, stadiums, famous skills and football jargon.
  • Check for spelling and correctness.
  • You can referesh the page to get a new distribution of the words (bear in mind this restarts progress)

Enjoy the game and test your footy knowledge in this corssword challenge!
